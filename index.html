Our Comprehensive Approach

ZoomRx's reputation tracking studies provide pharmaceutical companies with actionable insights into healthcare professional perceptions, competitive positioning, and strategic opportunities for brand enhancement.

🎯 Multi-Dimensional Perception Mapping

Assess your brand through HCP perceptions across five weighted dimensions—Innovation, Patient Centricity, Reputation, HCP Engagement, and Promotional Effectiveness—to derive an overall reputation score.

🏆 Competitive Landscape Analysis

Gain deep insights into your competitive landscape with detailed analysis of top performers, market gaps, and positioning opportunities. We track all the top manufacturers simultaneously to provide comprehensive market context.

💡 Strategic Action Planning

Leverage our analytical frameworks to develop targeted strategic actions for reputation enhancement. Our insights translate directly into actionable recommendations for innovation focus, engagement strategies, and competitive differentiation.

Analytical Frameworks

ZoomRx delivers actionable insights through specialized analytical frameworks tailored specifically for pharmaceutical reputation research needs.

📊 Reputation Matrix

Visualize your competitive position based on perceived portfolio strength and relationship equity among stakeholders. This matrix helps identify where manufacturers stand and guides strategic focus for reputation growth.

🎯 Driver Analysis Map

Identify the key drivers of manufacturer reputation by analyzing stated vs. derived attribute importance. This framework reveals hidden opportunities, latent drivers, and cost-of-entry factors, providing a clear roadmap for prioritizing reputational investments and strategic focus areas.

📈 Reputation Journey

Track how HCP perceptions evolve throughout their engagement journey with your brand, from initial awareness through ongoing relationship development and advocacy.
